About Morph

Founded in 2024 in Palangkaraya, Morph was created as a space for visual exploration, strategic design, and digital storytelling. We believe every brand has a shape, rhythm, and personality — our job is to help reveal it through design, motion, content, and interactive experiences.

What We Do

  • Brand Identity - Building visual identity from logo, color, and typography.
  • Web Design & Development - Modern, responsive, and fast websites.
  • Social Media Creative - Visual campaigns and digital assets.
  • Campaign Direction - Concept, visual direction, and communication strategy.
  • Motion & Visual Assets - Motion graphics, posters, and experimental graphics.
